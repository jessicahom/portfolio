Projects

Beethoven's Drei Equali

With the brass section of Mary Lou's Apartment, I recorded this short piece, a pandemic collaboration on Ludwig van Beethoven's Drei Equali (Movement 2). We took it really fast because the pandemic is a time-warping experience.

P.A.W.S.

I used Javascript React and the PetFinder API to create Pet Assistance for Work Success, a site where an animal coach will help you get through your to-do list. The site allows you to choose a coach of the species of your choice from a shelter near you, and your coach will give you encouragement as you add, complete, and delete tasks from your list.

Standards-Based Grading

I conceptualized and implemented a version of standards-based grading in my 9th grade English class. Developed in collaboration with a professional learning community at Cal Prep, this system prioritized clarity of learning objectives, accuracy of grades in terms of reflecting true learning, and opportunities for growth and reflection on the part of students.
